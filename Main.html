MISSION

Nowadays, people are working at home or studying remotely.

People have issues on keeping track of time. They got distracted by multiple resources.

People have issues on keeping work-life-balance. Break time is not easy to keep up with.

Everything leads to procrastination and ends up in a bad cycle.

SOLUTION

Break down work into intervals of 25 minutes, which are separated by 5 minutess break.

Our app allows users to create a list of distractions and we will stop users from doing those activities.


Our app also includes features of other traditional time management tools, like calendar, reminder, and alarm clock
